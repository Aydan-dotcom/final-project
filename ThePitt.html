The Pitt:

The Pitt Ranking:

The Pitt is the only live-action show I am rating because I believe it is the best live-action show I've seen. The concept is simple, it is a show focused around workers (mostly doctors) of a hospital in Pittsburgh. The entire first season of the show, which is the only season out as of right now, only takes place in a single day, where a couple of new workers are employed from college.

1. Conflict-

The conflict of The Pitt is done very well, especially the last couple of episodes where every seen becomes suspenseful. The conflict is also very realistic and not over the top like most other series of emergency workers. I rate the conflict an 8/10.

2. Characters-

I love most of the characters of the show. Obviously it is much easier to have good characters in live action because there is a real person acting as them, but still the characters feel real, funny, and best, just as flawed as average people. I rate the characters a 9/10.

3. Plot-

The plot of The Pitt is very simple and easy to understand. There can't be much said about the plot. 6/10.
